# Weekday Baking Schedule

For a same-day bake, mix the dough early in the morning and keep the dough warm enough to ferment steadily. Perform three or four rounds of stretch-and-folds during the first two hours of bulk fermentation.

Once the dough looks lively and elastic, preshape it, rest it for twenty minutes, and complete the final shape. If the dough is fermenting quickly, it can proof at room temperature and be baked the same afternoon.

For a more flexible schedule, bakers often refrigerate the shaped loaf overnight. Cold proofing improves flavor and makes scoring easier the next day.
